## What is Showtimes NG?

Showtimes NG is Lagos' premier cinema guide that aggregates movie showtimes from all major cinemas across the city. Find out what's playing at Filmhouse, Genesis, Silverbird, and more - all in one place.

Instead of visiting multiple cinema websites, Showtimes NG brings all the information you need together, making it easy to compare showtimes, find the perfect viewing time, and discover what's currently showing in Lagos.

## What Information You'll Find

For each movie, Showtimes NG provides:

- **Movie Details**: Title, poster, description, and release year
- **Ratings**: IMDb, Metacritic, and Rotten Tomatoes scores
- **Runtime**: Movie duration in hours and minutes
- **Showtimes**: Available viewing times grouped by date and cinema
- **Screen Types**: 2D, 3D, IMAX, and other formats
- **Cinema Locations**: Venue names and addresses across Lagos
- **Booking Links**: Direct links to purchase tickets

<Note>
  Showtimes NG aggregates data from major Lagos cinemas including Filmhouse, Genesis Deluxe Cinemas, Silverbird Cinemas, and more.
</Note>
