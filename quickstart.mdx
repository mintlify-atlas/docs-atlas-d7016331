## Find Your First Movie Showtime

This guide will walk you through using Showtimes NG to find a movie, view showtimes across Lagos cinemas, and book your tickets.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Current Movies">
    Visit the Showtimes NG homepage to see all movies currently showing in Lagos.

    The homepage displays a grid of movie posters with key information:
    - Movie title
    - Duration (e.g., "2h 15m")
    - Ratings from IMDb, Metacritic, and Rotten Tomatoes

    <Tip>
      Movies are sorted by their earliest available showtime, so you'll see films starting soonest at the top.
    </Tip>

    Click on any movie poster to view detailed information and available showtimes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Movie Details">
    On the movie page, you'll find:

    - **Poster and Synopsis**: Large movie poster with full description
    - **Release Information**: Year, runtime, and ratings
    - **Multiple Ratings**: See scores from IMDb, Metacritic, and Rotten Tomatoes at a glance

    Example of what you'll see:
    ```
    Year: 2024
    Duration: 2h 28m
    Ratings:
      IMDb: 8.5
      Metacritic: 78
      Rotten Tomatoes: 92%
    ```

    Scroll down to the "Showtimes" section to see when and where the movie is playing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Date">
    Use the interactive date picker to choose when you want to watch the movie.

    The date picker shows:
    - **Day of week** (e.g., "MON", "TUE")
    - **Date number** (e.g., "15")
    - **Month** (e.g., "MAR")

    <Note>
      The current or nearest available date is selected by default. Click any date button to see showtimes for that day.
    </Note>

    Dates are displayed horizontally and you can scroll to see more options in the coming days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Cinema">
    After selecting a date, you'll see showtimes organized by cinema.

    Each cinema listing shows:
    - **Cinema name** (e.g., "Filmhouse IMAX Lekki")
    - **Location** (e.g., "Admiralty Way, Lekki Phase 1")
    - **Available showtimes** displayed as clickable buttons
    - **Screen type** indicators (e.g., "3D", "IMAX")

    Example showtime display:
    ```
    Filmhouse IMAX Lekki
    Admiralty Way, Lekki Phase 1

    [11:30 AM]  [2:15 PM]  [5:00 PM]  [8:30 PM (IMAX)]
    ```

    <Tip>
      Click on the cinema name to see all movies currently showing at that location.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Book Your Tickets">
    Click on any showtime button to be directed to the cinema's official booking page.

    <Warning>
      Ticket booking is completed on the cinema's website. Showtimes NG provides direct links to make the process seamless.
    </Warning>

    When you click a showtime:
    1. A new tab opens to the cinema's booking page
    2. The specific movie and showtime are pre-selected
    3. Complete your purchase on the cinema's secure checkout

    Different screen types (2D, 3D, IMAX) are clearly indicated, so you know exactly what format you're booking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browse by Cinema

If you prefer to browse by location rather than movie:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Cinemas Directory">
    Navigate to the "Cinemas" section to see all cinema locations across Lagos.

    Each cinema card displays:
    - Cinema name
    - Location or area (e.g., "Victoria Island", "Lekki", "Ikeja")

    Cinemas are listed alphabetically for easy browsing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Cinema">
    Click on any cinema to view:
    - Full address details
    - All movies currently showing at that location
    - Available showtimes organized by date

    This is useful when you:
    - Already know which cinema you want to visit
    - Want to see what's playing nearby
    - Are comparing multiple locations in the same area
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Movie">
    Browse the movies showing at your selected cinema.

    Showtimes are grouped by movie, showing:
    - Movie title (clickable to view full details)
    - All available times for your selected date
    - Screen type options

    Click any showtime to book your tickets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Showtime Information

### Screen Types

Showtimes NG displays different screen formats:

- **2D**: Standard digital screening (default, not always labeled)
- **3D**: Three-dimensional format requiring 3D glasses
- **IMAX**: Large-format premium screens with enhanced audio
- **4DX**: Motion seats with environmental effects (where available)

<Note>
  Screen type indicators appear next to showtime buttons when the format is not standard 2D.
</Note>

### Cinema Chains

Showtimes NG aggregates data from major cinema chains including:

- **Filmhouse Cinemas**: Multiple locations including IMAX Lekki
- **Genesis Deluxe Cinemas**: Premium locations across Lagos
- **Silverbird Cinemas**: Established chain with various formats
- And more venues across Lagos

## Tips for Using Showtimes NG

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check Multiple Dates" icon="calendar-days">
    If your preferred showtime is sold out or inconvenient, use the date picker to explore other days. Weekend showtimes often have more options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compare Cinemas" icon="location-dot">
    View the same movie across different cinemas by checking the full showtime list. Some locations may have more convenient times or preferred formats.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Look for Ratings" icon="star">
    Use the IMDb, Metacritic, and Rotten Tomatoes scores on movie cards to help decide what to watch. Higher scores generally indicate better-received films.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Book Early" icon="clock">
    For popular movies or prime showtimes (evenings and weekends), consider booking in advance as seats fill up quickly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
